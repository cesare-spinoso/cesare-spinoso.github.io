Exercise. Find regular expressions that satisfy the following statements. Assume that \( \Sigma = \{a, b\} \).
  1. Find regular expressions \( r, s \) such that \( L(rs) = \{a\} \).
  2. Find regular expressions \( r, s \) such that \( L(r + s) = \{a, b\}^* \).
  3. Find a regular expression \( r \) such that \( L(r^*) \) is finite.
